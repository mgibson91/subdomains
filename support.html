Common Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your data lives in your personal iCloud account. We never see or store it on our servers.
How do I sync between parents?
Go to Settings → Invite a Star Giver. Both parents need to be signed into iCloud.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Can I delete all my data?
Yes. Go to Settings → Reset Everything, or simply delete the app.
